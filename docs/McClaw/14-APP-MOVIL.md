# 14 - Mobile App (iPhone and Android)

## Status: Research

## Goal

Explore the development of an iPhone and Android app that communicates with McClaw.

---

## Topics to Investigate

- Communication between the mobile app and McClaw (protocol, synchronization)
- Technology: native (Swift/Kotlin) vs cross-platform (Flutter, React Native, etc.)
- Minimum features for the mobile app
- Connection model: direct (LAN), remote (internet), or both
- Possibility of the mobile app functioning as another McClaw Channel
