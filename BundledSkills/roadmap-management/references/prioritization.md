# Prioritization Frameworks

## RICE Score
RICE = (Reach x Impact x Confidence) / Effort

## MoSCoW
Must have, Should have, Could have, Won't have.

## ICE Score
ICE = Impact x Confidence x Ease (each 1-10)

## Value vs Effort Matrix
- High value, Low effort: Quick wins (do first)
- High value, High effort: Big bets (plan carefully)
- Low value, Low effort: Fill-ins (if time allows)
- Low value, High effort: Money pits (avoid)

## Dependency Mapping
- Technical, Team, External, Knowledge, Sequential
- Assign owner, set "need by" date, build buffer
